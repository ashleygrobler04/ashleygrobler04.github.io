Welcome to the online BMI calculator

Hi and welcome to the Online BMI calculator.
Have you ever been asked what your BMI is, but did not totaly have the resources to calculate it?
Well it is easy to calculate your BMI when you've got your calculator with you, but do you always remember the formula?
If you don't remember the formula, sure you can always google it, and make use of it.
But that all sounds like a bit of work, doesn't it? Don't worry, this online BMI calculator has you covered. It is easy to use, usable from any where and is totaly accessible to screen reader users.

How does this work?

You will be presented with 2 edit boxes, where you enter the required information.
After entering your information, click on the "calculate" button, and your browser will do the magic work, and display your BMI!

Start calculating!
